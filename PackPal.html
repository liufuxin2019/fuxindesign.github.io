PackPal

An Automatic Weighing Packaging Machine.





Basic Information

Responsibility Range：mechanical design and construction, 3D modeling, business model analysis.
Team Members：SHI Xiahao, LIU Hangtao, SUN Hanyun, XIE Ziyang
Director：HU Gailing
Duration：2016.03 - 2016.05









Background

With the advent of the "Internet +" wave and people's growing concern about health issues, nutritious natural grains such as millet have begun to be more popular and attracted. Many farmers responded to the call of the times and started to sell cereal products online. The micro-commercial sales of cereals are mostly called by artificial means, and the steps are complicated; the grain is easily scattered, and it is almost impossible to recycle after being sprinkled. After market research, it is found that most of the existing packaging machines of the same type have complicated operation and high price, and many functions are not used by small merchants. Compared with the purchase of such expensive large packaging machinery, small merchants are more willing to use traditional labor-intensive operations for traditional packaging operations.
